How We Craft

Precision from grain to glass.

We build depth through careful choices—time, temperature, toast, and taste.

  • Slow, cool fermentation for cleaner aromatics
  • Copper pot distillation for textured spirit
  • Proofed with mineral-balanced water for a soft finish
Sourcing Traceable grains and botanicals from trusted growers. Mash & Ferment Controlled temps preserve nuanced esters and mouthfeel. Distill & Rest Selective cuts, then patient barrel time for integration.

Closed-loop cooling and heat recovery reduce our water and energy use.

Signature Cocktails

Bar-worthy, home-easy builds.

Three- to five-ingredient recipes that respect the spirit and the moment.

Ember Old Fashioned 2 oz Smoked Rye, 0.25 oz demerara syrup, 2 dashes bitters. Stir with ice, strain over rock, orange twist. Golden Bee Sour 2 oz Honey Cask Bourbon, 0.75 oz lemon, 0.5 oz honey syrup, egg white optional. Dry shake, shake with ice, strain. Cinder Highball 1.5 oz Citrus & Juniper Gin, 0.5 oz grapefruit, top with chilled soda. Build over ice, long grapefruit peel.

Always measure responsibly; adjust sweetness and dilution to taste.
